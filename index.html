Who I am

Frontend Developer | Plant Fanatic

I’m Tamás, a frontend developer based in Rome with a background in business and a curiosity-driven mindset. What started as a creative hobby quickly grew into a focused journey into web development. I’m drawn to clean design, thoughtful user experiences, and the process of turning ideas into something real and usable.

Originally from Hungary, I’ve studied, worked, and lived in four different countries: Hungary, Italy, Spain, and the Netherlands. That international experience has shaped how I think, how I work with others, and how I approach challenges. I speak four languages, enjoy the rhythm of remote work, and recharge by taking care of plants and simplifying the systems around me — both digital and personal.

I’ve been learning to code through the Scrimba Frontend Developer Career Path — a self-paced, project-based program that helped me grow my skills through real practice, not just theory. While it’s not a formal university course, it provided a hands-on environment where I built full projects using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. It taught me how to think like a developer, troubleshoot independently, and confidently bring ideas to life in the browser.
